GUIDE

How to use the calculators

General Instructions

1

Input Section Properties

Enter the dimensions or select a standard shape from the selection.

2

Define Material & Length

Specify the yield strength (Fy) and the length (L) of your member.

3

Select Analysis Method

Toggle between LRFD or ASD to apply the appropriate safety factors.

4

Review Results

The calculator will instantly display the design strength and indicate if the member passes the limit state checks.

5

Check for Warnings

Look out for "Failed" indicators, which suggest the member did not pass the required specifications.
